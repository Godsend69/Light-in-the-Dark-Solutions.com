Deployments

The Deployments feature makes shipping new versions a breeze. Here’s what you get:

  • One-Click Deploy: Publish new versions instantly, with full rollback support.
  • Deployment History: Track every release, who deployed it, and when.
  • Status Monitoring: Get live feedback on deployment progress and results.
  • Safe Rollbacks: Instantly revert to any previous stable version if needed.
  • Team Collaboration: Permissions and notifications keep everyone in sync.

How to Use

  1. Go to the Deployments section on your dashboard.
  2. Click ‘Deploy’ to start a new release, or view history for past deployments.
  3. Set up notifications for deployment completions or issues.
