Notifications

Notifications keep you informed and responsive. Features include:

  • Real-Time Alerts: Instant updates on system status, deployments, and user activity.
  • Customizable Channels: Choose email, in-app, or Slack notifications for different events.
  • Priority Levels: Highlight critical issues so you never miss what matters.
  • Alert History: Review past notifications and action logs for auditing.
  • DND & Mute: Silence non-urgent alerts during focus time.

How to Use

  1. Open the Notifications panel on your dashboard.
  2. Set your preferences for channels and alert types.
  3. Review, acknowledge, or mute notifications as needed.
