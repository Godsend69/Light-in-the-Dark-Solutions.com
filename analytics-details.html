Analytics

With Analytics, you unlock powerful insight into your app’s usage and growth. Our dashboard gives you:

  • Real-Time User Metrics: Instantly see how many users are active, what they're doing, and top trends.
  • Engagement Tracking: See which features are used the most and where users drop off.
  • Custom Reports: Filter and download reports for deep-dive business insights.
  • Funnels & Retention: Visualize onboarding success and user retention over time.
  • Privacy-First Data: All analytics are aggregated and anonymized for GDPR compliance.

How to Use

  1. Visit your dashboard and select ‘Analytics’.
  2. View summarized stats, trends, and export data as needed.
  3. Set up custom alerts for key metrics.
