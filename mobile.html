How to Install:
1. Tap Download Android APK above.
2. If prompted, allow downloads from this site.
3. Open your Downloads and tap the APK file.
4. If you see a warning, go to Settings → Security → Install unknown apps and enable it for your browser.
5. Confirm and install.

Your phone may show a security warning. Only install apps you trust.
Prefer not to download?
You can use OurApp like a native app without installing anything.
  • Open this page in your phone’s browser.
  • Tap Menu (⋮ or ☰) or Share (🔗).
  • Choose Add to Home Screen.
  • OurApp icon will appear on your phone like a regular app!
